MINDSET 2.0 is a structural macroeconomic model developed for research and
policy analysis. This page gives a short orientation; for full technical
detail, see the reports listed below.

## Documentation

Complete documentation of MINDSET 2.0 is published on SSRN:

- [Model Documentation](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=6188978) — structure, equations, and assumptions.
- [Data Documentation](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=6206398) — data sources, construction, and series.
- [Estimation Documentation](https://papers.ssrn.com/abstract=6213498) — estimation strategy, identification, and diagnostics.

You can also browse all reports from the [Reports page]({{ '/reports/' | relative_url }}).
