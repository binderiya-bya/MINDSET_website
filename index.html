The MINDSET Project

A global macroeconometric model for climate, development, and trade policy analysis.

MINDSET 2.0 evaluates how climate change, climate policies, and other development and trade policies affect macroeconomic, sector-level, and labour market outcomes — through a demand-driven, recursively dynamic macroeconometric framework.

Explore the model View publications

About MINDSET 2.0

MINDSET has been applied to a wide range of developing economies for distributional consumption and employment analysis. Academic and government institutions across Europe, North America, Latin America, and Central Asia are currently extending MINDSET to assess energy transitions and climate mitigation policies.
