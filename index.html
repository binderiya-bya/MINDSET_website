The MINDSET Project

A global macroeconometric model for climate, development, and trade policy analysis.

MINDSET 2.0 evaluates how climate change, climate policies, and other development and trade policies affect macroeconomic, sector-level, and labour market outcomes — through a whole-of-economy, people-centred framework.

About MINDSET 2.0

MINDSET stands for Model of Innovation in Dynamic Low-Carbon Structural Economic and Employment Transformations. MINDSET 2.0 is a global, demand-driven, recursively dynamic macroeconometric simulation model. It covers 163 countries and regions with 120 sectors in each, and links to country-level labour force and household survey data to analyse impacts across income strata, skill levels, occupation types, and sub-national provinces.
